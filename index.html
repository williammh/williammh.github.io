About

Born in Seattle, I was a child drawn to activities that involved building with technology. This ranged from creating custom game mods for Starcraft: Brood War to writing early websites using Notepad and Photoshop. After graduating from The University of Arizona with my bachelor of science in public policy, I returned home to the Pacific Northwest and began teaching myself more modern methods of web development.

Today, I offer my services on the consulting team at ConnectWise, personally ensuring our software meets the demands of hundreds businesses. Though when not billing hours, I may be the learning new technologies, weightlifting, playing PC games, or watching documentaries. I consider myself a big science enthusiast and highly recommend these favorites.
